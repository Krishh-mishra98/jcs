Continueing Rehabilitation Programme(CRE)

The Organization conduct CRE program for the Rehabilitation council of India (RCI) registered professional/personal special educators. It is 5 days, 3 days or Seminar. Workshop are also six-month online program which is approved by RCI, New Delhi. The purpose of this CRE program is to update special educators working for disabled children and renewal of RCI registration for the working professional/personal.

The Organization has conducted 03 CRE at the center, 30 participants in each batch have been attended CRE program in academic session 2019-20.

Early Intervention

The Sansthan is organized an early intervention program for the children with disability at the age of 02-03 years’ parents of disabled children encouraged to bring their children for skill development and readiness for schooling. Our target is troubleshooting of the children with skill not develop under the program.

Human Recourse

The organization is human resource development center for the hearing handicapped and intellectual disabled. This program is recognized by Rehabilitation council of India, New Delhi. Under the program we are providing Diploma in special education Hearing handicapped and intellectual disabilities (D.Ed. SE(HI)and D.Ed. SE (ID).It is two years’ program designed by RCI. This diploma is equal to BTC of the state government and candidates have an opportunity to get employment in the general education under the state government. The admission of the candidates in diploma is subject to Rehabilitation Council of India(RCI), New Delhi its terms & condition for each academic year. The intake capacity of the organization is 30 D.Ed.SE (HI) & 30 D.Ed. SE (ID) approved by the council.

Examination and certification is provided by the NBER (National Board of Exam in Rehabilitation) of the Rehabilitation Council of India. The RCI have authorized to the “National Institute of the Government of India” to conduct diploma level examination on the behalf of NBER,RCI New Delhi and released the marksheet and certificate to the candidates.

About Us

Introduction

The Jan Chetna Sansthan is a registered society, registered under society act 1860 (21) in 04-04-1996 in Allahabad Utter Pradesh with registration No. 03/1996-97. The Organization has started to work for the intellectual Disabilities (ID), Cerebral Palsy, (CP), Autism, Multiple Disabilities and Hearing Handicapped Children. The Sansthan is also registered with National Trust Act 2099-2000 to work with the above children The Sansthan is also registered under PWD Act 1995.

The organization is running SAMARTH Scheme of the National Trust and the program is 100% national Trust as per SAMARTH Scheme. It is also running a special school for hearing handicapped children atid intellectual disable namely “Jagat Narayan Viklang Sansthan” and a teacher’s training center (Diplomain Special Education (ID) & (HI) recognized by Rehabilitation Council of India, New Delhi.

The SAMARTH Scheme is running a separate rented building at 201/1E, Prayag Puram Naini, Prayagraj and special school and teacher’s training center program in separate rented building.

Legal status of the Organization

The Organization registered under society registration Act 1860(21).

Registered under PWD Act 1995.

The registered under 12AA of income tax department.

The Organization have 80G certificate of income taxdepartment.

Annual income tax return (ITR) under income tax department.

Registered under National Trust Act 1999 the Organization.

The Organization recognized by Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI) New Delhi.

Aims & Objective of the Organization

The Organization is working with the following aims & objectives:

To work for the disabled (Divyangjan) population.

To provide special education to the needy children.

To develop skills of the disabled children.

To conduct the community awareness program about disabled (Divyangjan).

To organize camp, Seminar, Workshop, for the information about disable person.

To rehabilitate disabled children in the society for the main streaming.

To develop vocational skills for the self-employment & self confidence in the disabled person.

To provide parents guidance and counseling for their child development.

To conduct program for Psychical, Mental, Communication skills, Language, personality etc.

To develop human resources (Man Power) for the disable children.
